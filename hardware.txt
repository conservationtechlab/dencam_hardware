# Cameras

[Raspberry Pi High Quality Camera with C-mount](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-high-quality-camera/)

Specs for current model in use:
> 12.3 megapixel Sony IMX477 sensor, 7.9mm diagonal image size, and back-illuminated sensor architecture, with adjustable back focus.
> C/CS mount
> Raspberry Pi High Quality Camera will remain in production until at least January 2030

Note that the cable supplied with the camera should work with the Pi 4, but a different cable is needed for Pi Zero.



# Lens Adapter

> The High Quality Camera CS Mount variant is designed to accept CS-mount lenses and, with the supplied adapter, C-mount lenses. 
> [...]
> ...[see step-by-step instructions](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-high-quality-camera/) for fitting these CS-mount and C-mount lenses.

So, you will need an adapter from your lens to C-mount body type.

- [Vello LA-C-NF Nikon F Lens to C-Mount](https://www.vellogear.com/product/12571/Vello-LA_C_NF-Nikon-F-Lens-to-C_Mount-Camera-Lens-Adapter)
